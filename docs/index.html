What We Built

Krishak Seva helps small and marginal farmers make better decisions about water, crops, diseases, and market prices — without needing an agronomist in every village.

🌱

Personalized Crop Advice

Season-based recommendations (Kharif / Rabi / Zaid) using soil, weather, and groundwater data.

🌦️

Weather & Irrigation

Live forecasts, dry-spell alerts, and practical irrigation guidance tailored to your crop.

🔬

Crop Doctor

Scan leaf photos with AI to detect disease, get treatment steps, and organic remedies.

🎙️

Bilingual Voice AI

Ask farming questions in English or Telugu — by voice or text — and get spoken answers.

📡

Alerts & SOS

Push notifications for farm risks; web platform adds SMS, IVR helpline, and emergency SOS.

💧

Groundwater Intelligence

District-level CGWB data from OpenCity.in enriches crop suggestions in water-stressed areas.

Two Platforms, One Mission

Mobile-first for farmers in the field; web platform for dashboards, IVR simulation, and broader reach.

Mobile App

कृषकसेवा Android

A production-oriented Android app built for real village conditions — offline-friendly, Telugu-first UI, and secure AI through Firebase Cloud Functions (API keys never on device).

  • Phone OTP & guest login with farmer profile
  • Pincode auto-fill (village, district, state)
  • Smart dashboard: weather, water score, crop health
  • AI crop recommendations with groundwater enrichment
  • Camera / gallery disease detection (Crop Doctor)
  • Voice Q&A in English & Telugu
  • Push notifications for farm alerts
Kotlin Jetpack Compose Firebase Google Gemini Room Hilt CameraX
Web Platform

Krishak Seva Web

A full-stack web companion with farmer registration, live mandi prices, crop advisor, AI chatbot, IVR helpline simulator, and emergency SOS — deployable on Google Cloud Run.

  • OTP farmer login & GPS-based registration
  • Live weather + 7-day forecast (Open-Meteo)
  • AI crop comparison & profit calculator
  • Leaf disease diagnosis (photo + voice)
  • Bilingual floating AI chatbot (Groq Llama 3.1)
  • IVR phone simulator + Twilio outbound calls
  • SOS broadcast with GPS & regional helplines
Flask Vanilla JS Groq AI Twilio SoilGrids API Cloud Run
